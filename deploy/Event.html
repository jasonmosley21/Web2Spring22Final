Upcoming Event

The Grand Bookseller Swap

Saturday, February 26 from 1-4 PM

The lovely booksellers of A Novel Idea & The Spiral Bookcase — Christina and Victoria, respectively — have grown a bit stir-crazy in their little shops during the winter, so they’re orchestrating a grand swap!
On Saturday, February 2 from 1-4 PM, Christina will be slinging books at The Spiral Bookcase, located in Manayunk. The shop will be stocked with all of Christina’s favorites and recommendations — including her latest release, Creole Conjure.
And then on Saturday, February 26 from 1-4 PM, we’ll be playing it in reverse with Victoria slinging books at A Novel Idea, located in East Passyunk. All of Victoria’s most beloved reads will be on the shelves for recommendations to fly.

The Selkie Book Club

The Boy With a Bird in His Chest

Wednesday, March 3 at 7:30 PM EST

Join us for the virtual gathering of Spiral's Selkie Book Club! Focused on magical adult fiction, this book club meets on the second Wednesday of each month at 7:30 PM EST via Zoom. Dive deep into Victoria's selection of stranger worlds, shimmering doors, supernatural quests and mystical landscapes.

Worm Moon Salon

Saturday, March 19 at 7 PM

Join us for a virtual version of our monthly Full Moon Salon meetings! Open to all on the path of witchcraft & the occult, this discussion-based, virtual event is a friendly place for folks to chat about witchcraft, paganism, the occult, astrology, meditation and other folklore practices.

A Weekend with Bharat Krishnan

Saturday, April 2 & Sunday, April 3

The Spiral Bookcase is honored to present a weekend of writing workshops with author Bharat Krishnan. Focused on novel-writing, these two workshops — available to be taken together or separately — will guide you through imbuing your work with emotional honesty and authentic diversity. Packed with exercises, handouts and engaging conversations, this weekend provides hands-on tools for any writer working to ensure their manuscript is all that it can be.
